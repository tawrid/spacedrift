Welcome to Space Drift: Mind & Motion

In the vast, silent stretches of space, keeping the mind sharp and the body active is a challenge unlike any other. Space Drift: Mind & Motion is more than just a game—it's a journey designed to help you stay engaged, entertained, and healthy while navigating the depths of the cosmos. Whether you're on a long voyage or simply searching for new ways to challenge yourself, this game brings the fun back into focus.

Test Your Skills

Test your skills as you guide a ball through ever-changing tubes, dodging obstacles and defying the forces of space. With each movement, you'll be sharpening your reflexes, boosting your concentration, and keeping boredom at bay. But it’s not just your body that benefits—your mind will be constantly working to predict, strategize, and overcome the challenges ahead.

Physical and Digital Versions for Every Explorer

Space Drift is available in two formats—physical and digital. The physical version lets you engage directly with the game, offering a tactile experience to improve hand-eye coordination and motor skills. The digital version immerses you in a futuristic environment, with dynamic levels that evolve as you progress. Both versions are crafted to help you feel grounded, even in the vast emptiness of space.
