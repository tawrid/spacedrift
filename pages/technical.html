Technical Specifications

The technical foundation of Space Drift: Mind & Motion is designed to create an engaging and versatile gaming experience. Below are the key components and specifications:

1. Collapsible Tube Design

1.1 Structure and Materials

  • Material: Lightweight and durable materials, such as reinforced PVC or carbon fiber.
  • Extended Length: 2 meters
  • Collapsed Length: 0.5 meters
  • Diameter: 30 centimeters (fixed)

1.2 Mechanism

The tube features a collapsible design utilizing interlocking segments, allowing it to be easily extended or collapsed:

  • Collapsing Mechanism: Push or pull to extend/collapse with a secure locking mechanism for stability during gameplay.

2. Adjustable Obstacles

2.1 Types of Obstacles

  • Rectangular Blocks:
    • Standard Dimensions: 10 cm (height) x 20 cm (width) x 5 cm (depth)
    • Adjustability: Minimum size of 5 cm x 10 cm x 2.5 cm, maximum size of 20 cm x 40 cm x 10 cm.
  • Spheres:
    • Standard Diameter: 10 cm
    • Adjustability: Minimum diameter of 5 cm, maximum diameter of 15 cm.

2.2 Placement and Arrangement

Obstacles can be positioned and arranged to increase complexity:

  • Levels 1 to 5: Minimum gap of 30 cm, maximum gap of 60 cm between obstacles.
  • Levels 6 to 10: Minimum gap of 15 cm between obstacles, increasing challenge.

3. Level Complexity

The game consists of ten levels with increasing complexity based on:

  • Levels 1 to 5: Simpler arrangements with fewer obstacles.
  • Levels 6 to 10: More obstacles, increased ball size from 5 cm to 10 cm, enhancing maneuvering difficulty.

4. Conclusion

The technical design of Space Drift: Mind & Motion integrates innovative features to promote an engaging and challenging gameplay experience while ensuring safety and usability in a microgravity environment.
