Purpose

The purpose of **Space Drift: Mind & Motion** is to address various challenges faced by astronauts and space travelers. These challenges include:

  • Space Radiation: Understanding and mitigating the risks of radiation exposure in space environments.
  • Isolation and Confinement: Addressing the psychological impacts of being isolated from Earth and confined in a spacecraft.
  • Distance from Earth: Exploring ways to combat the feeling of disconnection from our planet during long missions.
  • Gravity Fields: Adapting to the effects of microgravity on physical health and performance.
  • Hostile/Closed Environments: Developing strategies to maintain well-being in environments that are restrictive and potentially hazardous.

Through engaging gameplay, this project aims to enhance mental fitness and physical well-being, helping space travelers thrive in their extraordinary journeys.
