How It Works

Space Drift: Mind & Motion offers a unique gameplay experience that combines fun with mental and physical challenges. Here's how it works:

Gameplay Mechanics

  • Objective: Players must navigate a ball through a transparent tube filled with various obstacles, including rectangular blocks and spheres, without touching them.
  • Levels: The game consists of multiple levels (L1 to L10), each increasing in complexity and challenge.
  • Controls: Players use intuitive controls (keyboard or touch interface) to maneuver the ball within the tube.
  • Obstacles: Different shapes and sizes of obstacles are introduced as players progress through levels, requiring strategic thinking and quick reflexes.

Level Progression

As players advance through the levels:

  • Levels 1 to 5: Simpler obstacles allow players to learn the mechanics and improve their skills.
  • Levels 6 to 10: The size of the ball is reduced, increasing difficulty and requiring players to adapt to new challenges.

Physical and Digital Versions

The game is available in two formats:

  • Physical Version: Players interact with a tangible setup that enhances hand-eye coordination.
  • Digital Version: Offers an immersive experience with dynamic visuals and sounds, making it engaging in a virtual environment.
